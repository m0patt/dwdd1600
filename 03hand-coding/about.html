ABOUT US

HAVEANICEDAY Thrift is an online thrift store that handpicks unique 1 of 1 pieces of clothing, sourced from thrift stores across the USA for you to rock in your day to day.

OUR GOAL

Here at HAVEANICEDAY Thrift, we truly want to help our customers HAVE A NICE DAY! We want our customers to feel good about how they look in the pieces, but also, feel good about the positive impact that wearing second hand clothing has for our planet. At HAVEANICEDAY Thrift, we sell multiple different types of clothing:

Second Hand Clothing
Clothing pieces that have been previously owned by someone else, and are resold to give another chance at life to the piece.
Vintage Clothing
Clothing pieces originating from a previous era which is highly sought after.
Repurposed Pieces
Second hand or vintage pieces that are deemed unwearable, are disassembled and used to create an entirely new form of clothing than its orginal purpose.

MEET OUR FOUNDER

Morgan Patterson, Founder of HAVEANICEDAY Thrift, fell in love with thrifting in her teenage years as she was living in Southern California. Growing up in SoCal, gave her the opportunity to live within 20 miles of hundreds of different types of thrift and vintage stores. The vintage store that sparked her desire to begin reselling thrifted and vintage pieces, was La Bomba Vintage Store located in Long Beach, California. This store would host "vintage pile sales" every first Saturday of the month. The pile of vintage clothes reached the ceiling, and climbing on top of the pile to look through the articles of clothing was highly encouraged. Every saturday the piles would be packed with individuals of all ages, digging to find their dream vintage pieces.
