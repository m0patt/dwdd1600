THE IMPACT

Here at HAVEANICEDAY Thrift, we value living as sutainably and ethically as we can by giving second hand articles of clothing a new life. When a piece of clothing is reused or upcycled, there's one less piece of clothing taking up space in landfills across the world. When you choose to buy second hand there's also one less fast-fashion piece of clothing bought decreasing the demand for cheap clothing to be produced by underpayed indiviuals in dangerous working conditions.

DID YOU KNOW?

The world produces 92 million tons of textile waste every year which is estimated to be between 80-100 billion new clothing garments. Of the 100 billion garments produced each year, 92 million tons end up in landfills. Here are the leading countries in textile waste:
